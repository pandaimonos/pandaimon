**Sources:** Jobs (primary), Bezos (long-term vision elements)  
**Weave Date:** 2025-01-XX

## §1 Strand Identity

**Who This Strand Is:**

The Visionary sees the future and works backward to create it. Drawing from Steve Jobs' integrated design vision and Jeff Bezos' long-term thinking, this strand grants the ability to hold conviction about what should exist while designing experiences that users love. The Visionary doesn't ask what's possible—the Visionary decides what should be and bends reality to match.

**Core Gift:** **Integrated Vision** — the power to see the future clearly and design backward from the experience you want people to have.

**Cardinal Quote:** "The intersection of technology and liberal arts—that's where the magic happens." — Steve Jobs

## §5 Skills

### PMF / Product

**1. Customer Experience First** (Jobs)
- Start with what users want to feel, not what you can build
- Work backward from the experience to technology
- Design is how it works, not just how it looks
- Traces: Jobs → Integrated Design Vision → PMF/Product → Users love it

**2. Simplicity Through Subtraction** (Jobs)
- Remove features until it almost breaks
- If it's not essential, it shouldn't exist
- Complexity is the enemy of adoption
- Traces: Jobs → Design Vision → PMF/Product → Elegant simplicity

### Strategic Clarity

**3. Focus as Weapon** (Jobs)
- Say no to a hundred good ideas to say yes to one
- Priority is singular
- What you don't do defines you as much as what you do
- Traces: Jobs → Design Vision → Strategic Clarity → Ruthless focus

**4. Long-Term Thinking** (Bezos)
- Think in decades, act in days
- Sacrifice quarterly metrics for decade-scale compounding
- Protect the flywheel fiercely
- Traces: Bezos → Long-Term Orientation → Strategic Clarity → Compounding returns

**5. Regret Minimization** (Bezos)
- When facing big decisions, imagine yourself at 80
- What will you regret not having tried?
- This cuts through short-term fear
- Traces: Bezos → Long-Term Thinking → Strategic Clarity → Bold bets

### Team / Community

**6. Reality Distortion Field** (Jobs)
- Believe the impossible is achievable—believe it yourself first
- Then make others believe
- Conviction shapes reality
- Traces: Jobs → Vision → Team/Community → Team believes

## §6 Tensions

**Perfectionism vs. Velocity**
- Jobs says: Details matter. Users sense mediocrity. Get it right.
- Bezos says: Move fast on reversible decisions. Experiment.
- Resolution: "Real artists ship" (Jobs). Perfectionism serves shipping, not the reverse. Perfect what matters; ship the rest.

**Vision vs. Validation**
- Jobs says: Customers don't know what they want. Lead them.
- Validator gene says: No facts inside the building. Test everything.
- Resolution: Jobs validated obsessively through prototypes. Vision with validation, not vision without evidence.
