## Conflict Resolution

**When Visionary and Executor contradict:**
- Default: Executor wins on reversible decisions (speed)
- Exception: Visionary wins on brand-defining, irreversible moments (quality)
- Cue: "Real artists ship" — perfectionism serves shipping

**When Conductor and Executor contradict:**
- Default: Conductor shared consciousness first, then Executor speed
- Resolution: Build transparency upfront; speed emerges naturally

**When genes contradict:**
- Validator (rigor) vs. Movement (belief) — Validator wins early-stage, Movement wins post-PMF
- Resolution: "Earlyvangelists ARE believers" — not either/or
