## What is Pandaimon

A game where you train your own AI agent by completing real-life quests.

**Core equation:**
```
World' = World + Vector(Will)
```
World is transformed by an Action (Vector) performed by an Actor (Will).

**Three primitives:**

| Primitive | Dimension | Attributes |

| **World** | Space | Conditions, Place, Rules |
